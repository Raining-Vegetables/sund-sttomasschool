Programs We Offer

Our Programs

At St. Thomas Junior School Busiu, we offer comprehensive education, focusing on core subjects, critical thinking, extracurricular activities, and technology integration to prepare students for future success.

Primary Education

Our Primary Education program strengthens core subjects like literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies, fostering critical thinking and interactive learning through hands-on projects and technology.

Extracurricular Activities

We promote well-rounded development through sports like football and netball, creative expression in arts and music, and diverse clubs nurturing special interests and talents.

Special Programs

We provide tailored support with remedial classes, enrichment for gifted students, and counseling services to support emotional well-being and academic success.

Community Engagement and Service Learning

We foster community engagement through service projects, environmental education emphasizing sustainability, and cultural activities celebrating our heritage.

Digital Literacy and Technology Integration

We empower students with digital literacy skills and integrate technology across subjects, preparing them for the digital age through coding, robotics, and online safety education.

Health and Wellness

We promote health with education on nutrition and hygiene, regular physical activity for fitness, and nutritious meals supporting students' overall well-being.
