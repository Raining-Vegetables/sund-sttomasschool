Welcome to St Thomas Nursery & Primary School

Welcome to St. Thomas Nursery & Primary School! Located in the heart of Mbale town in Eastern Uganda, St. Thomas Nursery & Primary School is dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. Our school offers a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential. Join us on a journey of discovery, growth, and excellence. We are excited to be part of your child's educational journey and look forward to creating lasting memories together.
