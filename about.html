About Us

More About Us

At St. Thomas Junior School, we are committed to providing scholarships, fostering professional development, and delivering impactful education that shapes bright futures for our students through the following aspects

Scholarships and Support
We offer scholarships to students with academic potential but lacking financial resources. Our program addresses financial challenges, ensuring every child can achieve their educational goals.
Professional Development
We invest in continuous professional development for our teachers. Regular training, workshops, and educational resources equip them with the latest teaching methodologies and best practices.
Our Impact
Since 2010, we have impacted Busiu's education. Our graduates excel in higher education and professions. They contribute positively to communities.
Join Our Family
Join the St. Thomas Junior School Busiu family. Parents, students, educators, and partners are welcome. Help us nurture young minds and inspire innovation.

Academic Programs

Our academic programs are designed to provide a balanced and comprehensive education. We offer:

  • Early Childhood Education: Laying a strong foundation in the early years.
  • Primary Education: Building on foundational skills with a focus on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Offering diverse opportunities in sports, music, arts, and clubs to develop well-rounded individuals.
