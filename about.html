Welcome to St. Thomas Junior School Busiu

St. Thomas Junior School Busiu is a cornerstone of education in the serene community of Busiu, Mbale. Since our inception in 2010, we have been dedicated to providing a nurturing and innovative educational environment that promotes the holistic development of our students.

More About Us

Welcome to St. Thomas Junior School Busiu's commitment to excellence and support. Discover how our scholarships, professional development, and impactful education shape bright futures.

Scholarships and Support
We offer scholarships to students with academic potential but lacking financial resources. Our program addresses financial challenges, ensuring every child can achieve their educational goals.
Professional Development
We invest in continuous professional development for our teachers. Regular training, workshops, and educational resources equip them with the latest teaching methodologies and best practices.
Our Impact
Since 2010, we have impacted Busiu's education. Our graduates excel in higher education and professions. They contribute positively to communities.
Join Our Family
Join the St. Thomas Junior School Busiu family. Parents, students, educators, and partners are welcome. Help us nurture young minds and inspire innovation.

Our Core Values

At the heart of St. Thomas Junior School Busiu are our core values that guide everything we do:

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and personal conduct.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Integrity: Promoting honesty, respect, and responsibility among students and staff.
  • Community: Building strong, supportive relationships within and outside the school.

Educational Philosophy

Our educational philosophy is centred on the belief that each child is unique and deserves personalised learning experiences. We focus on:

  • Individual Attention: Catering to the specific needs and abilities of each student.
  • Holistic Development: Encouraging growth in academics, sports, arts, and social skills.
  • Lifelong Learning: Instilling a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Academic Programs

Our academic programs are designed to provide a balanced and comprehensive education. We offer:

  • Early Childhood Education: Laying a strong foundation in the early years.
  • Primary Education: Building on foundational skills with a focus on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Offering diverse opportunities in sports, music, arts, and clubs to develop well-rounded individuals.
