Welcome to St. Thomas Junior School Busiu

At St. Thomas Junior School Busiu, we are dedicated to fostering a conducive learning environment that promotes individual learning for each of our students. Located in the serene community of Busiu, Mbale, our mission is to provide quality education that empowers young minds and inspires innovation.

Our Programs

The following are the programs from our school.

Academic Excellence.
Our curriculum is designed to challenge and engage students, preparing them for higher education and beyond.
Extracurricular Activities.
We offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and music, to develop our students' talents and interests.
Student Support Services.
We provide support services to help students with their academic and personal development, including counselling and mentoring.

About Us

Founded in 2010, St. Thomas Junior School Busiu was one of the first schools in our remote village to offer quality education. Our commitment to excellence has made a significant impact on the level of education in the community. We believe that every child deserves access to high-quality education, regardless of their financial background.

Our Mission

Nurturing young minds, inspiring innovation. We aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared for the challenges of the future. Our mission is to provide an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of quality education in the community, fostering a love for learning and innovation in our students. We strive to improve the quality of our educators through continuous training and professional development, ensuring they are well-equipped to support each student's learning journey.

Scholarships
